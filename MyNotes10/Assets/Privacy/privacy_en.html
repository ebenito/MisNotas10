Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 4, 2025

Thank you for using MisNotas10. This app is designed to help you manage your personal notes easily and securely. Below we explain how your information is handled.

1. Information collected by the app

MisNotas10 does not collect, store, or share personal information with the developer or with third parties. All data created by the user is stored locally on the device.

2. Use of OneDrive

The app offers an optional feature to backup and restore your notes using your personal Microsoft OneDrive account.

  • Access to OneDrive is used only to save and retrieve a backup file containing your notes.
  • The app does not access or view any other files or folders in your OneDrive.
  • You can revoke this access at any time from your Microsoft account settings.

3. Data security

Your notes are stored on your device or in your private OneDrive space. No data is transmitted to external servers or to the developer.

4. User rights

Since MisNotas10 does not collect personal data, it is not necessary to exercise rights of access, correction, or deletion. You have full control over your data directly within the app and through your OneDrive account.

6. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the app. You will be informed of any important changes through the usual app distribution channels.

7. Contact
